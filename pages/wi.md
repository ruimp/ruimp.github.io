The **Weekly Invariant** is a discord group for sharing and discussing interesting problems. Our aim is to create a friendly but driven community to share a passion for solving and thinking about beautiful problem in diverse areas such as mathematics, physics and computer science.

Join us on [Weekly Invariant](https://discord.gg/uaqMRTU) and discover the joy of problem solving and learning with the support or our community.
